====== Algrex Barbs ======
Often found on beaches along the shores of [[:Ensulasia]], Algrex barbs are the remains from dead [[:fauna:Algrex]]. Algrex barbs are quite a plentiful ingredient, due to the prevalence of Algrex in the oceans, and do not spoil once collected making them a standard in any [[:magic:potions|potion]] makers store cupboard.

From Algrex barbs, it is possible to extract a few different forms of essence. They can be ground into Algrex barb dust or alternatively disolved in vinegar over a few weeks to create a concentrated Algrex barb extract.
