====== The Grey Dog ======
The most popular pub among locals in [[:places:Tharmouth]], The Grey Dog is often full of workers and soldiers alike at the end of a long day. It is a frequent spot for many of the guards of the empire. [[:people:Mazoon]] is a travelling bard who often spends evenings in The Grey Dog performing for locals.

The innkeeper is an old man, [[:people:Frinly]], who has owned the bar for the better half of his life.
